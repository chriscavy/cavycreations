Meet my Piggies!

Molang & PiuPiu

Two years ago I adopted these two from the local Humane Center. They had come in from separate families and were alone. It's always a gamble getting two boars, but it has worked out well. Molang (the fluffy one) was originally named "Yoda" and the short-hair was originally named "Gordo." I'm a big fan of the cartoon Molang and decided to name them after the best friends Molang and PiuPiu from the show. They are both very loving and well-behaved!
